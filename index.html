Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

App Name: Focus Warrior

Developer: Staffone Christian

1. Introduction

Focus Warrior ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected and how it is handled.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • No account registration is required.
  • No personal identifiers such as name, email, or phone number are collected.
  • We do not operate our own user database.
  • User data is not sold.
  • Users are not tracked across other apps or websites.

3. Information Stored by the App

Local Device Storage

Focus session data, coins, and app progress are stored locally on your device.

iCloud Storage

If iCloud is enabled on your device, focus session data and coin balances may be securely stored and synchronized using Apple's iCloud services.

  • This data is stored in the user’s private iCloud account.
  • The developer does not have access to this data.
  • Data is controlled by the user’s Apple ID.
  • Apple manages iCloud data according to its own privacy policies.

Apple Privacy Policy:
https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

4. Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage information, including:

  • App launches
  • Screen views
  • Feature usage
  • Session duration

This data:

  • Is anonymous
  • Is not linked to your identity
  • Is not used for advertising
  • Is used only to improve app performance and user experience

5. In-App Purchases (If Applicable)

If the App offers premium features, purchases are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store payment information.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Analytics (Google)
  • Apple iCloud Services

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

Google Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

7. Children’s Privacy

Focus Warrior is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Security

We use secure communication (HTTPS) and rely on Apple’s secure iCloud infrastructure for cloud synchronization.
